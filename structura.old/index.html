Details

The movie shows the distant sky cut up into thin slices, shown as if we were flipping through the pages of a book. Now let's focus on the main chapters to learn more about the fascinating objects in the slices. The slideshow below starts with the familiar night sky and takes you all the way to the Big Bang.

Click the right arrow or swipe to begin.

A Map of The Universe

This is a map showing what astronomers see in the sky. You are located at the corner at the bottom, and the observable edge of the Universe is shown at the top. At the edge is the the cosmic microwave background, was emitted 13.7 billion years ago. In between, astronomers have detected millions of galaxies and supermassive black holes.
