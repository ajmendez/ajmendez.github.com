Details

The movie shows a "flipbook" of the sky, constantly moving backwards as individual pages of a book. Lets dig into the main chapters of this book of the universe to learn more about the structures. The slideshow below will start with the night sky that you are familiar with and take you back to the Big Bang. Click the right arrow or swipe to start.

The Universe

Rather than focusing on the "individual pages" of the book of the universe, looking at the spine of the book allows us to see a slice through the universe. The top of this slice starts at first flash of light, the cosmic microwave background, and covers the 13.7 billion years to today at the bottom. In between astronomers have identified millions of galaxies and quasars, used to understand the changes in the universe, the growth of structure, and understand how gas is used by stars and quasars.
